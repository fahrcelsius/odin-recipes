Mie Ayam Recipe

Description

Mie Ayam Pangsit is a delicious Indonesian homemade noodle recipe with chicken and fried dumplings. This week, we bring you a fabulous Indonesian recipe for noodles with chicken and fried dumplings. Mie Ayam Pangsit contains a garlic sauce that adds an exquisite flavor to the noodles. I'm sure you'll love it!

Ingredients

For Chicken:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into small squares
  • 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 3 red onions, chopped
  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • salt to taste

For Garlic sauce:

  • 8 garlic cloves, grated
  • 8 tablespoons pork skin oil or sautéed chicken
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth

For noodles:

  • 1 pack egg noodles
  • 1 Lettuce or spinach or some green vegetables
  • 1 Leek and onion, finely chopped
  • 1 chopped green onion

Step by step to cook mie ayam

To cook chicken:

  1. Heat the oil, add the garlic and onion. Come out until golden brown.
  2. Add chicken, sweet soy sauce, soy sauce, salt and chicken broth.
  3. Let it simmer until most of the broth is gone.

To cook the garlic:

  1. Heat the oil.
  2. Add garlic, soy sauce and chicken broth.

To cook the noodles:

  1. Boil the noodles and lettuce.
  2. Fry the leek and onion.

At the time of serving:

  1. In a separate bowl, add the garlic sauce and mix with the noodles.
  2. Place the lettuce in a serving bowl, then add the noodles.
  3. Put the chicken and meatballs with the noodles.
  4. Add chicken, leeks and fried onions on top of the noodles.
  5. Add the green onion.
  6. Finally add some broth to taste.

Enjoy your meal!
